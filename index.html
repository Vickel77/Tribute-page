Dr Miles Munroe

Dr Miles Munroe profound author, speaker and leadership consultant

In honor of a legend

Our hearts are very heavy today. Recently, Pastor Myles Munroe, founder and leader of Bahamas Faith Ministries International (BFMI) and Myles Munroe International (MMI), was killed when his plane crashed in the Bahamas while on his way to Freeport, Grand Bahama for a conference. His wife and three other ministry leaders were also killed in the crash.

From humble beginnings, Munroe was born and raised in the Bahamas, became a Christian when he was a teenager, and went on to attend Oral Robert University where he received a Bachelor of Fine Arts, Education and Theology. He later went on to receive a Master's degree in administration from the University of Tulsa, and upon graduation, returned home to the Bahamas to found Bahamas Faith Ministries International in the early 1980's.

The author of many best-selling books, Munroe was best known for his charismatic, inspirational and motivational speaking. He encouraged other to live their lives with purpose, and exemplified this encouragement in his own life. He will be greatly missed by all those whom his life has touched.
